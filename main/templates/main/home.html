Who we are

We are a small earn-to-give startup that specialises in Federated Bookkeeping. All our projects are open source and we aim to connect existing systems with each other, rather than introducing yet another alternative.

Projects

We build connectors that allow computer systems to federate with each other. Possible first projects may include Odoo and ERPNext plugins for joining Peppol, and a Nextcloud plugin for joining ScienceMesh.
